What is the process?

How and why it’s important for you to take the anxiety test

1

START THE TEST

Before you can start your test it's necessary that you carefully read the general information on the test page, there we specified that the test you are about to perform should NOT serve as a replacement for a real diagnosis made by a trained professional, however, results can help you to take the next step on your journey of beating anxiety.

2

ANSWER THE QUESTIONS

We will ask you to answer 21 questions with complete honesty, each one of them must be answered in the shortest possible time. It's important that you try not to over analyze the questions, do not think too much about it.

3

REVIEW YOUR RESULTS

By default your results will be public, you can share them with anyone you want and anyone can access them as long they have the correct URL. When you are a registered user your results will become private and will be stored with the date on which you took the test so you can come back as many times as you want to keep track of them.
